Architecture is the art and science of designing buildings and other physical structures. It plays an important role in shaping the environment in which we live, work, and interact. Good architecture combines beauty, functionality, and safety to create spaces that are both useful and visually appealing.
Architects use creativity along with technical knowledge to plan and design buildings. They consider factors like space, materials, climate, and purpose before creating a structure. Architecture is not just about construction—it also reflects culture, history, and technological progress. There are different types of architecture, such as residential architecture for homes, commercial architecture for offices and malls, and landscape architecture for outdoor spaces like parks and gardens. Each type serves a unique purpose but follows the same principles of design and structure.
